About

MRTNS Equities

MRTNS Equities is a Belgian private equity real estate firm founded by Nicolas Mertens. Since 2012, we have focused exclusively on residential development in Antwerp's prime urban markets.

Our investment thesis is straightforward: acquire well-located properties below replacement cost, develop high-quality residential units, and create value through disciplined execution. We are owner-operators — involved from acquisition through delivery.

Entrepreneurship

Owner-led decision making. Speed and conviction in every transaction.

Discipline

Track Record

Focus

One market. One asset class. Deep expertise in Belgian residential development.
